About

We're making Stanford an even better place for entrepreneurs.

health++ is all about empowering students to change healthcare. We aim to promote and cultivate health entrepreneurship on campus by creating a forum for developers, entrepreneurs, and pre-health students to collaborate. As an undergraduate initiative, we want to create an environment to kickstart innovation and empower entrepreneurship in health technologies.

We're looking for people who share our vision to join us in making Stanford an even better place for student entrepreneurs. We invite you to actively brainstorm with us and join us as an early-stage visionary by contacting us below with ideas that can motivate student entrepreneurship in this area!

The mission

Allowing these groups of students to collaborate creatively

Pre-health

These are students who are passionate about healthcare and know it needs fixing. We integrate their identified needs in a collaborative effort to turn them into solutions.

Developers

The developer community is full of hackers, designers, and creators who want and can make a difference. We're challenging them with some of the most important and interesting problems out there.

Entrepreneurs

Skilled at building, prototyping, and rapidly iterating, these students drive and inspire innovation in their own circles. We combine their skills with the resources and the right people to get the job done.
