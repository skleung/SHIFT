About

health++ is all about empowering students to move healthcare forward. We aim to promote and cultivate health innovation on campus by creating a forum for developers, entrepreneurs, and pre-health students to collaborate. As an undergraduate initiative, we want to create an environment to equip students for the growing intersection of healthcare and technology.

Join us in turning the page to the next chapter of healthcare. Contact us below with ideas, suggestions, or just to get in touch!

Join Us!

The mission

Allowing these groups of students to collaborate creatively

Pre-health

These are students who are passionate about healthcare and know it needs fixing. We integrate their identified needs in a collaborative effort to turn them into solutions.

Developers

The developer community is full of hackers, designers, and creators who want and can make a difference. We're challenging them with some of the most important and interesting problems out there.

Entrepreneurs

Skilled at building, prototyping, and rapidly iterating, these students drive and inspire innovation in their own circles. We combine their skills with the resources and the right people to get the job done.
