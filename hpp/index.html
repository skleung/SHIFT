health++ Fellowship
Project-based opportunities to learn by doing

health++ fellows receive the opportunity to immerse themselves in a quarter-long project tackling high-impact problems in the healthcare sector. Fellows work closely with interdisciplinary teams and an industry or Stanford-affiliated liaison throughout the quarter.

Interested? Apply to join the 2015 cohort!

Projects

Fall 2015

Asian Liver Center

An open roundtable discussion to introduce and expose students to areas in health care. We put industry leaders together with a diverse group of students with different perspectives to health care. Rather than having a speaker and an audience, these think-tanks are set up to foster an open conversation for people to learn and explore a space collaboratively.

Project Kraeplin

A fellowship program that pairs students as health++ fellows with early-stage companies or faculty-sponsored projects. Fellows will be organized into student teams with a variety of skill sets to collaborate on a clearly defined project in the healthcare space. Industry or faculty mentors then work closely to guide students in a journey of learning by doing.
